# PROMPTS USED

## AI Summary Generation Prompt
"Generate a concise, professional summary of AI tool spending optimization results for a startup engineering team. Highlight savings, inefficiencies, and recommendations."

## Audit Explanation Prompt
"Explain AI tool cost optimization recommendations in simple business language suitable for founders."

## Edge Case Handling Prompt
"Summarize audit results even when minimal or incomplete tool data is provided, focusing on safe recommendations."

## Tone Constraint
All outputs must remain professional, concise, and non-technical for business users.
